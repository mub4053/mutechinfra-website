Clear answers to your questions

Sterling Abode is a premium residential apartment project featuring five floors, with each floor comprising two spacious apartments. The project is situated on a 457-square yard plot and has received approvals from GHMC. The apartments are available in two sizes: 1740 sq ft and 1550 sq ft, and are designed by the renowned "MIA Architects." Located in Budvel, near Janachaitanya Colony, close to PVNR Expressway Pillar No. 215, Sterling Abode offers excellent connectivity to key areas like Gachibowli, Banjara Hills, Mehdipatnam and Shamshabad. The location is also adjacent to the upcoming 200 ft road and surrounded by the lush greenery of Acharya NG Ranga Agriculture University, which ensures pure air and a slightly lower temperature.

Sterling Abode provides flexible payment options to suit different needs. Buyers can opt for an EMI plan, starting with a small booking amount, followed by a percentage of the cost within a week, and the rest spread over 12 monthly installments. Alternatively, the One-Shot Payment option allows for upfront payment, often with a negotiable discount. These options ensure a convenient and tailored approach to home buying.

Sterling Abode offers a unique combination of advantages that set it apart. The project provides spacious and well-designed apartments with modern amenities, ensuring a comfortable living experience. Its strategic location within walking distance of the upcoming 200 ft road across the Musi River enhances connectivity, while the flexible payment options make homeownership more affordable and accessible. The exclusivity of having only two apartments per floor ensures privacy, and the project is surrounded by a vibrant neighborhood in Janachaitanya Colony, known for housing elite businessmen and NRIs. Additionally, its proximity to the expansive 2000-acre Acharya NG Ranga Agriculture University provides residents with pure oxygen and a cooler, greener environment year-round.
