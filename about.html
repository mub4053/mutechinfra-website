about us

Bringing Innovation And Heart to Every Detail

Founded and incorporated in the year 2020 by Mohd. Mushtaq Ahmed, a civil engineering professional with vast and diversified experience, MUTECH INFRA PROJECTS LLP, is currently into construction and development of commercial & residential multistoried buildings, integrated residential townships, farmhouses & resorts and industrial buildings, delivering innovative, appealing and quality projects.
Since its inception and successful execution of construction projects of diverse nature, the company has grown significantly whilst still maintaining the professional values instilled by the managing partners.
The company has always embraced the changes within the industry and thrived on innovation, continuous improvements and best practices, and is developing into a dynamic and futuristic company. The company is managed by highly qualified professionals who are fully engrossed to ensure that it maintains high standards in construction quality, timely delivery and client satisfaction. The group has always strived hard to keep its commitments and thus enjoys an extremely sound reputation in the construction industry with a close interaction with clients, consultants and buyers by fulfilling their aspirations. The company enjoys a sound financial base, infrastructure and has requisite experienced man power, machinery and equipment to deliver quality projects within specified time.
